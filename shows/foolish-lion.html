Foolish Lion & Clever Rabbit

About the Show

An exciting tale of wit and wisdom where a clever rabbit outsmarts a mighty lion. This engaging puppet show teaches children about intelligence, courage, and the power of quick thinking.

Show Details

  • Duration: 40 minutes
  • Age Group: 4-12 years
  • Language: English/Hindi
  • Cast: 3 puppeteers

Educational Value

  • Teaches problem-solving skills
  • Promotes creative thinking
  • Shows the importance of intelligence over strength
  • Encourages bravery and quick thinking
