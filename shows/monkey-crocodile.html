Monkey and The Crocodile

About the Show

A delightful adaptation of the classic tale about friendship, trust, and wisdom. Join our lovable monkey and the clever crocodile in this heartwarming story that teaches valuable life lessons.

Show Details

  • Duration: 45 minutes
  • Age Group: 4-12 years
  • Language: English/Hindi
  • Cast: 3 puppeteers

Educational Value

  • Teaches the importance of friendship
  • Promotes critical thinking
  • Encourages problem-solving skills
  • Develops emotional intelligence
